PLATFORM CAPABILITIES

End-to-End Disaster Management

From early detection to community recovery — a complete suite of tools to monitor, coordinate, and respond to disasters.

Real-time Monitoring

24/7 monitoring of weather events, seismic activity, and developing situations with instant push notifications.

Impact Analytics

Comprehensive dashboards with population impact, damage assessment, and resource allocation insights.

Evacuation Management

Track facility capacities, monitor occupancy rates, and coordinate shelter operations across all centers.

Relief Coordination

Track food, non-food, and financial aid distribution to ensure equitable and efficient resource delivery.

DROMIC Reports

Generate standardized DROMIC situation reports with automated data aggregation and one-click export.

Population Tracking

Detailed sex-age disaggregated data and sectoral distribution for targeted and inclusive response.
