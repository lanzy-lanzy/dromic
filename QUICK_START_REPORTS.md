# Quick Start: Comprehensive Reports Feature

## What's New?

Your Reports Dashboard now has a professional PDF export feature that generates a comprehensive disaster response report.

## How to Use

### Step 1: Access Reports Page
Go to: `http://127.0.0.1:8000/reports/`

### Step 2: Click Export Button
- Look for the blue **"Export Report"** button in the top right
- Click it to download the PDF

### Step 3: Open the PDF
- File name: `comprehensive_disaster_report.pdf`
- Opens with your default PDF reader
- Professional, printable format

## What's In the Report?

### Section 1: Executive Summary
Quick overview of all key metrics:
- Total disasters and affected areas
- Number of families and persons affected
- Evacuation center capacity
- Displacement statistics
- House damage summary
- Relief assistance totals

### Section 2: Disaster Breakdown
Table showing impact by disaster type:
- Disaster name and category
- Families affected
- Persons affected

### Section 3: Demographics
Sex and Age Distribution:
- Male/Female breakdown
- Age groups (Infant, Child, Adult, Senior)
- Cumulative vs. Current numbers

### Section 4: Sectoral Distribution
Population by special groups:
- Senior citizens
- Children
- Out-of-school youth
- Solo parents
- PWD (Persons with Disabilities)
- Indigenous peoples
- Farmers/Fisherfolk

### Section 5: House Damage
Summary of property damage:
- Partially damaged houses
- Totally damaged houses
- Total damage count

### Section 6: Relief Operations
Assistance provided:
- Food items distributed
- Non-food items distributed
- Financial assistance (₱)

### Section 7: Evacuation Centers
Shelter statistics:
- Total centers operational
- Total capacity
- Current occupancy
- Available capacity

### Section 8: Displaced Population
Movement tracking:
- Families displaced (cumulative vs. current)
- Persons displaced (cumulative vs. current)

## Troubleshooting

### PDF Download Issues
1. Check your internet connection
2. Ensure browser allows PDF downloads
3. Check download folder for the file

### Missing Data in Report
- Report shows data that exists in the database
- Add more data through the application to populate sections
- Create disasters, affected areas, and relief operations

### Permission Denied
- Only logged-in users can export reports
- Sign in to your account first
